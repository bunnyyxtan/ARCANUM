# DAO Treasury Outreach

Subject: Your treasury keeper agents on Arc

Arc is shaping up to be a natural home for DAO treasury agents that hold, move, and earn USDC.

We built Arcanum as an open governance layer for those agents: on-chain spend ceilings, vendor allowlists, council escalation, and anomaly defense, all enforced by a GuardedWallet contract on Arc.

The integration is a three-line SDK change around an agent's existing USDC transfer path. The protocol is MIT, self-hostable, and non-custodial. Your multisig keeps custody. Arcanum just gives the agent wallet an enforceable policy envelope.

If you are exploring treasury keepers on Arc, I would love to share the Helix DAO demo and get your feedback.
