# Builder Badge / Arc House Submission

## Project Summary

Arcanum is open, non-custodial protocol-layer infrastructure for autonomous AI agent money on Arc. It gives agent wallets on-chain policy envelopes, human escalation, anomaly defense, and vendor allowlists while keeping custody with the user-owned wallet.

## Why Arcanum Fits Arc House

Arc is becoming the home for stablecoin-native builders, and autonomous agents are one of the clearest places where that matters. Arcanum contributes an open governance layer for those agents, built directly on Arc and designed around permissionless deployment. It helps DAO, DeFi, DePIN, and agent-framework builders ship safer USDC workflows while keeping custody and control in their own contracts.
